Beyonce : The Queen of Music

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter, simply known as Beyoncé, is a well-known popular singer, who has received a great deal of success with her incredible voice. When it comes to music,
there are few artists who have had as much of an impact on the industry as Beyonce. She is regarded as one of the most influential artists of the current music era.


Early Life

The young Beyonce born on the 4th of September 1981, in Texas, United States, was a child gifted with an incredible voice and a huge interest in music. With time her face gradually became
apparent with her appearance in local talent shows. Beyonce who joined singing in the church choir at a very young age was capable of performing in a girl's band at the age of nine.

Girl's Tyme, the girl's band was able to grab the attention of some significant music production companies. Girl's time officially signed the label to Columbia Records in 1997 and evolved with it's
new name Destiny's Child. The very Desiny's Child released their debut album under the same name as the band. Even though the "Destiny,s child" album was unable to gain considerable recognition, their very next album "The Writing's on the Wall", reached a massive hit, with an unexpected number of sold copies.

After the success of "The Writing's on the wall", the group was able to give continuous hits namely, "Survivor", "Independent Women", "Bootylicious", "8 Days of Christmas", "Girl" and "Lose My Breath".

“Destiny’s Child will always be No.1 on my list.”

Solo Career

After a few years in 2003 Beyonce's debut solo album "Dangerously in Love" became a massive success, with millions of copies sold worldwide. In 2013 her self-titled album hits
"Drunk in Love", and "Partition" was at number one on Billboard 200 chart.

When it comes to Beyonce's commercial hits, "Lemonade" is one of the most important albums to be specified. The album expressed and explored important themes such as feminism and black
Identity.

Apart from her solos, her "Everything is Love", which was a collaborative album with her husband, had a massive reach.

For her incredible hits throughout her career, Beyonce has accomplished a number of awards. She is known to be the most awarded female artist at the Grammy awards with 28 grammy awards.
Apart from this, MTV Video Music Awards, BET Awards, Billboard Millennium Awards, and CFDA Fashion Icon Awards, are the major awards Beyonce has received.



Activism

Apart from being a talented singer, She is also a well-known voice in the black lives matter movement and LGBTQ+ rights. She was courageous enough to address certain politically debatable topics like the issues black women face and police brutality and was fearless to question the government through her albums. Also, the fact she donated $6 million to coronavirus relief was one of Beyonce's important community services. As overall Beyonce is a popular voice, both in music and social issues.
